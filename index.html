About Us

ADAMAH’s unique product range is a brainchild of a black South African entrepreneur, Albanos Tshegofatso Mokoka. And as a vegetarian, Albanos, the founder of ADAMAH’s 100% plant-based product range believes that there’s a natural-link between the environment, the plants and human health (well-being). ADAMAH is a Hebrew word for THE SOIL/THE EARTH, because our entire product range, from our dried fruits to powder products, is 100% plant-based.ADAMAH is about introducing delicious, colourful and unique nutrients-dense high quality dried and powdered fruits & vegetables as well as powdered herbal plants with the ability to improve and better our health, to ordinary households, events, occasions, retail business and hospitality industry in South Africa, that can be enjoyed by both adults and children alike.

Our Vision

The core of our vision is to revolutionise the way ordinary people consume herbal plants for the betterment of their health, in South Africa and SADC region first and foremost. To grow from our humble beginnings, and be the largest 100% black-owned South African manufacturer of innovative & high-quality dried fruits & vegetables and powdered herbal plants, in South Africa, and the rest of the African continent. And, the first biggest milestone for ADAMAH business would be when our product range is exported to SADC and Middle-Eastern countries respectively. To contribute to SA’s job creation by creating decent employment opportunities for many South Africans.

Our Objectives

  • To revolutionise the way we consume herbal plants,
  • To provide a palatable and satisfactory nutrients-dense alternative snacks for those are health-conscious, as well as those with dietary restrictions or health limitations,
  • To use 100% locally produced raw fresh fruits and vegetables for our entire product range,
  • To manufacture preservative free, artificial colourants free and GMO-free product range.
